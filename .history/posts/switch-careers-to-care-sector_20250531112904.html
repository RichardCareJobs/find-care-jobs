How to Switch Careers into the Care Sector

Published 19 May 2025

The care sector is one of Australia's fastest-growing industries, and many people are making the switch from other fields. If you're considering a career change into aged care or disability support, here’s how to get started.

1. Identify Transferable Skills

Skills like communication, teamwork, time management, and problem-solving are all highly valued in care roles—even if gained in a different industry.

2. Explore Training Options

Consider enrolling in a Certificate III or IV in Individual Support. Some courses can be completed part-time or online and may include work placements.

3. Try Volunteering

Volunteering in a care setting can help you build confidence, gain experience, and decide whether the sector is the right fit for you.

4. Tailor Your Applications

When applying for entry-level roles, explain why you’re passionate about making the switch and how your background brings value to the team.

Switching careers takes courage, but the care industry welcomes people with heart, commitment, and the right mindset.
